VSH Hospital Management Website

The VSH Hospital platform is a dynamic web application designed to streamline hospital operations, enable seamless online bookings, and enhance patient engagement. It features advanced role-based dashboards, secure payment integrations, video consultations, and content management.

Built with Python Django, React, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the system empowers administrators, doctors, HR teams, and patients with dedicated tools and dashboards for efficient workflows.

Core Functionalities:
  • Department Management

    Admin can create, edit, activate/deactivate, and delete departments with real-time website updates.

  • Doctor Management

    Add and schedule doctors, manage availability, and provide a separate doctor portal for appointments and video consultations.

  • Appointment & Health Checkup Booking

    Patients can book services online with HDFC payment gateway integration and receive instant confirmations.

  • Video Consultation

    Secure video calls integrated into the doctor dashboard for online consultations.

  • Blog & Content Management

    Admin can create, edit, and moderate blogs, manage banners, galleries, and dynamic content.

  • HR Recruitment

    Post job vacancies, collect resumes, and manage applications from the HR dashboard.

  • Payment & Notification System

    View and filter payment transactions, and receive notifications for bookings and messages.

  • Role-Based Dashboards

    Separate views for Admin, Doctor, HR, and Patients for secure, organized access.
