Ara Skin Clinic Management Website

The Ara Skin Clinic platform is a dynamic web application designed to streamline clinic operations, enable seamless appointment bookings, and improve patient engagement. It features role-based dashboards, secure payment integrations via Easybuzz, and content management.

Developed using Python Django, React, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the system empowers administrators, doctors, HR teams, and patients with dedicated tools for efficient workflows.

Core Functionalities:
  • Department Management

    Admin can create, edit, activate/deactivate, and delete departments with real-time website updates.

  • Doctor Management

    Add and schedule doctors, manage availability, and provide a separate doctor portal for appointments.

  • Appointment & Health Checkup Booking

    Patients can book services online with Easybuzz payment gateway integration and receive instant confirmations.

  • Blog & Content Management

    Admin can create, edit, and moderate blogs, manage banners, galleries, and dynamic content.

  • HR Recruitment

    Post job vacancies, collect resumes, and manage applications from the HR dashboard.

  • Payment & Notification System

    View and filter payment transactions, and receive notifications for bookings and messages.

  • Role-Based Dashboards

    Separate views for Admin, Doctor, HR, and Patients for secure, organized access.
